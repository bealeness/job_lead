Welcome to job_lead

This website is dedicated to helping you sort and organise your job search. Here is a quick guide giving you the best information to get the most out of this website.

New Job
The most important feature of this website is the new job form. This is where you go to iniate a job in the job_lead system. Once saved it will appear as a new job on the Jobs page.

Jobs
The Jobs page is where all new jobs are listed. To see a more detailed view of each job simply click on the job role. You will be taken to the job detail page where you can either delete or update that job. Once you have applied for a job, it is your responsibility to update that job from 'fresh' to 'applied'. Doing this will move the job from the Jobs page to the Applied page. Also make sure to fill out the remainder of the update form. Write NULL if if something does not yet apply. Fill Interview Date in with today's date and update it when you do have an interview date.

Applied
This is where all of your applied for jobs are kept. Instead of displaying the deadline date, the applied for date is now shown. When you have received a response from the employer update the jobs status from 'applied' to 'response' and fill out the response act on the form.

Response
The response page is where all your responded to jobs are kept. The response is displayed in the far column. Once you have replied to the employer, update the jobs status from 'response' to 'replied' and fill out the reply act on the form.

Replied
The replied page is where all your replied to jobs are kept. The reply is displayed in the far column. If you are lucky enough to receive an interview from the employer update the jobs status from 'replied' to 'interview' and fill out the interview date and interview method sections of the form. If you are unsuccessful with receiving an interview you can simply delete the job and it will be removed from the job_lead system.

Interview
The interview page is the final stage of the job_lead process. This is where all of your interview details are kept including interview date and interview method.
